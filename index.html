Should I even do this?

A simple blog about a life in transition.

After law school, I decided not to practice law while I had little kids. Then I tried to go back into law a decade later, even took another bar exam. This blog chronicles my unlikely transition into software development, when law no longer seems like a good fit.

This is my first attempt at a website. I did just finish a full-stack web-development bootcamp this summer, but I didn't learn a ton about static webpage develpment. I did this over a weekend. My goal was to make it without using a paid hosting service like wix or square space because it was mostly just an exercise for me and not to a public site right now. I also wanted to challenge myself to modify some code, push it to github, and get it to production with netlify.
